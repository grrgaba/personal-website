Aurora Borealis

Edmonton, Alberta • Oct 10 2025

The night of October 10th brought one of the most intense and vibrant displays of the Aurora Borealis I've ever seen in Edmonton. The sky was absolutely electric.

The sky filled with electric purple and green.

Glowing ribbons passing over the power lines.

Looking straight up into the light.

The display lighting up the city skyline.

Reaching for the light.

Taking it all in together.

Green light contrasting with autumn leaves.

A front-row seat to the show.
